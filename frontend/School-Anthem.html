Our Pride

The St. Lawrence Anthem

Our school anthem is more than just a song – it's a powerful expression of our values, aspirations, and commitment to excellence. Every word resonates with the spirit of St. Lawrence Junior School, inspiring students to strive for greatness and uphold the principles that make our school exceptional.

Sung with pride at every school gathering, our anthem unites students, teachers, and the entire school community in a shared vision of academic excellence, moral integrity, and personal growth.

Our Heritage

The Story Behind Our Anthem

2010 - The Creation

When St. Lawrence Junior School was founded in 2010, our visionary founder Mr. Kimera Emmanuel recognized the need for a unifying anthem that would embody our school's values and aspirations.

The Composition

The anthem was composed by renowned Ugandan musician and educator Prof. David Musoke, who worked closely with our school community to capture the essence of St. Lawrence in every verse.

First Performance

The anthem was first performed at our inaugural graduation ceremony in 2011, bringing tears of joy and pride to everyone present. Since then, it has been sung at every major school event.

A Living Legacy

Today, our anthem continues to inspire generations of students, reminding them of their connection to St. Lawrence and the values we hold dear: excellence, integrity, and unity.
