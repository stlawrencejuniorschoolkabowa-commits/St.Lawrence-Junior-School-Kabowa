Our Approach

World-Class Education

At St. Lawrence Junior School, we provide a comprehensive and balanced curriculum that combines academic rigor with character development. Our teaching methodology focuses on developing critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills that prepare students for success in the 21st century.

We follow the Uganda National Curriculum with enhanced programs in Mathematics, Science, Languages, and the Arts. Our experienced teachers use modern teaching methods and technology to make learning engaging and effective.
